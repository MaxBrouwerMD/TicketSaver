All your tickets in one secure place.

TicketSaver keeps your event and travel tickets together in a private iOS app. You can add tickets manually, or let Gmail auto-forward selected confirmation emails into TicketSaver.

  • Store and organize tickets for events and trips.
  • Optional Gmail connect to forward specific confirmation emails.
  • Designed with privacy in mind – you stay in control of your data.
