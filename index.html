TicketSaver is a personal iOS app that helps you store and organize your event and travel tickets. You can add tickets yourself, or let TicketSaver forward specific confirmation emails from Gmail automatically.

TicketSaver uses the Gmail API only to manage forwarding and filter settings in your account – not to read inbox contents.

Gmail integration & data handling

TicketSaver uses restricted Gmail settings scopes only to configure:

  • a forwarding address pointing to your personal TicketSaver inbox,
  • a single Gmail filter that forwards matching confirmation emails, and
  • optional cleanup of these settings when you disconnect.

TicketSaver does not use the Gmail API to read or search your inbox. Email content is only processed for messages that you or your filters forward to TicketSaver, and it is stored per user in our backend so your tickets can be displayed in the app.
