Facebook User Data Deletion

If you signed in with Facebook and want your TicketSaver account data deleted, you can:

  1. Open the TicketSaver app → Profile → Delete account.
  2. Or email maxbrouwermd@gmail.com (from the address linked to your Facebook account) with subject “Facebook Data Deletion”.

Note: Deleting your TicketSaver account does not cancel an Apple subscription; manage billing in your Apple ID settings.
