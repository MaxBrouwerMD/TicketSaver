Facebook User Data Deletion

Last updated: October 26, 2025

This page explains how to delete data related to your use of Facebook Login with TicketSaver, and how we process deletion requests. It complements our Privacy Policy.

Fastest method: delete your account in the app

  1. Open the TicketSaver app.
  2. Go to Profile → Delete account and confirm.
  3. Your account and associated personal data are removed from our active systems promptly.
Important: Deleting your TicketSaver account does not cancel an Apple subscription. Manage or cancel it in your Apple ID settings: App Store → Account → Subscriptions.

Alternative: request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us at maxbrouwermd@gmail.com with the subject “Facebook Data Deletion Request”. To verify identity, please include one of the following:

  • The email address linked to your Facebook account; or
  • Your TicketSaver account UID (if known); or
  • A screenshot of your Facebook Apps and Websites page showing TicketSaver.

We will acknowledge your request and complete deletion from active systems without undue delay and no later than 30 days, subject to verification.

What we delete

CategoryExamples
Account profile Account identifier, login email (if available), authentication bindings (including Facebook login association), session/device bindings.
Content you provided Tickets and related metadata stored in our cloud (if any). Tickets stored only on your device are removed when you delete the app or files locally.
Notification & app signals Push tokens, in-app settings related to your account.
Facebook Login data Facebook auth association and tokens held by our auth provider (Firebase) for your account.

What may remain temporarily

  • Backups & logs: Routine system backups and server logs that are purged on rolling schedules.
  • Crash reports / diagnostics: Crash data (via Firebase Crashlytics) that typically expire after ~90 days and do not contain your ticket content.
  • Minimal anti-abuse records: Limited technical indicators (e.g., hashed device identifiers or receipt validation records) retained for a time to prevent fraud or repeated trial abuse, as described in our Privacy Policy and Terms.
  • Legal obligations: Data we must retain to comply with law or to resolve disputes.

Third-party services involved

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, storage, crash reporting, analytics (only with consent). Deletion removes your account and related records from active Firebase services.
  • Mailgun: If you used email forwarding, associated routing to your account is disabled when your account is deleted.
  • Apple App Store: Subscriptions are controlled by Apple on your Apple ID. Account deletion on TicketSaver does not cancel billing. Manage subscriptions in your Apple settings.

Revoke TicketSaver’s access on Facebook (optional)

In addition to deleting your TicketSaver account, you can revoke TicketSaver’s access in your Facebook settings: Facebook → Settings → Apps and Websites. This removes TicketSaver’s authorization from your Facebook account.
