Gmail Connect & Automatic Email Forwarding

Description of the Feature

TicketSaver offers an optional feature (“Gmail Connect”) that allows you to connect your Google Account and configure automatic forwarding of Gmail messages to your personal TicketSaver upload address (for example: upload+@upload.ticketsaver.nl).

To enable Gmail Connect, you must explicitly grant the App certain permissions via the Google consent screen. These permissions may allow the App to:

  • View and manage Gmail forwarding and forwarding addresses.
  • View, create, update, and delete Gmail filters that can move, label, or forward email.
  • View who else has delegated access to your Gmail and revoke such access.
  • View, create, update, and delete send-as aliases in Gmail.
  • View and update certain Gmail settings such as reply-to address, display name, signature, and automatic reply (vacation responder).
  • View and update IMAP/POP settings.
  • View your Google profile information and primary email address.

TicketSaver uses these permissions only as needed to configure and maintain automatic forwarding and to display your connection status, as further described in the Privacy Policy.
